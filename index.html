Welcome


The Medical Decision Research Lab studies how medical and health decisions are made, the factors that influence these decisions, and how the best decisions can be reached.


Join Us

By being part of the lab, you will gain experience with:

■ Running experiments
■ Collecting and managing data sets
■ Reviewing literature
■ Being a part of the scientific process

If you are interested in getting first-hand experience in the scientific process, please contact Dr. Victoria Shaffer. In the email please include if you have previous experience working in a lab, what school year you are currently in, whether you would like to volunteer or receive credit, and how many hours per week you can dedicate to our lab.
